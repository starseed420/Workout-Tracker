Hypertrophy Protocol

4-Day Routine

Coach Note: All working sets taken to 1 RiR or mechanical failure. Keep rest periods to 2-3 minutes for maximum neurological recovery. Timers default depending on neurological cost.
Monday
Shoulders, Arms & Abs
Seated Shoulder Press Maschine
3 sets x 6–8 reps
3:00
Keep elbows slightly tucked forward at a 30-degree angle to protect rotator cuffs. Control the descent.
Standing Cable Lateral Raises
4 sets x 10–12 reps
2:00
Set pulley at bottom position. Lean slightly away from stack to maximize bottom stretch.
Incline Dumbbell Bicep Curls
3 sets x 8–10 reps
2:00
Set bench to 45 degrees. Keep shoulders pinned against the pad. Lock elbows in place.
Cable Tricep Pushdowns
3 sets x 10–12 reps
2:00
Use independent or songle cable()s to pull past your hips at the bottom for a deeper tricep contraction.
EZ-Bar Preacher Curls
3 sets x 10–12 reps
2:00
Stop just short of total lockout at the bottom to maintain constant tension and protect tendons.
Overhead Cable Tricep Extensions
3 sets x 10–12 reps
2:00
Brace core, face away from stack. Focus entirely on extension at the elbow without drifting upper arm.
Friday
Legs
Hack Squat
3 sets x 6–8 reps
3:00
Place feet low on platform for deep knee flexion. Keep heels planted. Control the 3-second negative.
Seated Leg Curl
3 sets x 8–10 reps
2:00
Pull thigh pad down tightly against legs to lock pelvis. Squeeze hard at peak contraction.
Leg Press
3 sets x 10–12 reps
3:00
Foot placement low and shoulder-width for quad bias. Deep controlled range without lifting lower back.
Romanian Dumbell Deadlifts
3 sets x 8–10 reps
2:00
Soft bend in knees, push hips back as far as possible. Stop when hips stop moving backward to spare lower back.
Standing Calf Raises
4 sets x 12–15 reps
2:00
Drop heels completely, hold deep stretch for 2 full seconds to eliminate momentum, then explode up.
Saturday
Chest & Back
Incline Chest Press
3 sets x 6–8 reps
3:00
Use a modest 30-degree incline. Arch upper back slightly and drive feet into the floor for stability.
T-Bar Row
3 sets x 6–8 reps
3:00
Let shoulder blades fully stretch forward at the bottom, drive elbows back behind torso to finish.
Flat Machine Press
3 sets x 8–10 reps
2:00
Adjust seat height so handles align with mid-chest. Drive shoulder blades deep into the pad.
Neutral Grip Lat Pulldown
3 sets x 8–10 reps
2:00
Lean back slightly (10-15 degrees) and pull straight down to upper chest, driving down with elbows.
Machine Rear Delt Flyes
3 sets x 12–15 reps
2:00
Keep a slight bend in elbows and focus on pushing hands outward to the walls rather than pulling back.
Sunday
Sunday: Upper Body Hypertrophy
Very-Close Converging Chest Press
3 sets x 10–12 reps
2:00
Keep shoulders packed down and back. Focus entirely on pressing your biceps together at peak contraction.
Cable Pullovers
3 sets x 10–12 reps
2:00
Use a close neutral grip. Pull handle low toward your belly button, keeping shoulders dropped down.
Incline Cable Flyes
3 sets x 12–15 reps
2:00
Focus heavily on the deep, loaded stretch at the bottom where cables offer maximum continuous tension.
Lateral Raises
4 sets x 12–15 reps
2:00
Tilt torso slightly forward, raise dumbbells out to the sides in a wide arc. Keep pinkies slightly high.
Face Pulls
3 sets x 12–15 reps
2:00
Pull center of rope straight toward your nose, flaring elbows high and pulling hands apart at the back.
